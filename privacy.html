Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

Reta Australia (Amana Therapeutics) collects information necessary to provide our research services and products. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Full name and contact details.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage patterns on our site.
  • Communication Data: Records of your inquiries sent via our contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Fulfilling product orders and providing customer support.
  • Ensuring the safety and security of our encrypted communications.
  • Complying with Australian health and research regulations.

3. Discretion & Encryption

As noted on our contact pages, all communications are encrypted. We prioritize user discretion and do not sell or share personal data with third-party marketing firms.

4. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you or request its deletion. For any privacy-related concerns, contact our privacy officer at support@retaaustralia.com.au.
