Getting Started with Your Reta Australia Kit

Your kit includes a pre-loaded pen containing 10mg of retatrutide and 5 sterile needle caps. All components must be refrigerated between 2-8°C when not in use.

Starting Dose Protocol

Weeks 1-4: 2mg Weekly

  • Dial the pen to 16 clicks (8 clicks = 1mg)
  • Administer once per week, preferably in the evening
  • Rotate injection sites weekly to minimize tissue irritation

Administration Instructions

Injection Sites

Administer subcutaneously (into fatty tissue) in either:

  • Abdomen (avoiding 2 inches around navel)
  • Upper thighs

Important: Rotate sites each week to prevent tissue damage.

Step-by-Step Injection Process

  • Prepare the site: Clean injection area with alcohol swab and allow 30 seconds to air dry completely
  • Attach needle: Twist needle cap firmly onto pen until secure
  • Inject: Hold pen at 45° angle to skin, pinch fatty tissue with free hand, insert needle completely in one smooth motion, press plunger slowly and hold for 5 seconds after full depression
  • Remove: Remove needle and discard cap immediately in sharps container
  • Post-injection: Return pen to refrigerator and monitor site for 24 hours

Note: Minor redness or tenderness is normal; excessive swelling, prolonged pain, or signs of infection require medical attention.

Dietary Guidelines

Retatrutide slows gastric emptying, reducing appetite and extending satiety. To optimize results and minimize side effects, maintain these dietary practices:

Essential Requirements

  • Protein: Minimum 1.6g per kg of body weight daily. Include a protein source at every meal and prioritize lean proteins.
  • Hydration: 2+ liters of water daily to prevent constipation and support metabolic processes.
  • Fat moderation: Limit high-fat meals as excessive dietary fat combined with delayed gastric emptying may cause constipation.
  • Lifestyle factors: Minimize alcohol consumption and reduce or eliminate smoking.

Dose Adjustment Protocol

Weekly Monitoring (Required)

Track the following each week to inform dose adjustments:

  • Progress photos (same lighting, time of day, clothing)
  • Body weight (same scale, same time, consistent conditions)
  • Dietary adherence (protein intake, meal quality)
  • Appetite patterns (particularly on day 7 post-injection)

Understanding the 4-Week Evaluation Period

Why wait 4 weeks? GLP-1 levels accumulate gradually over several weeks. Initial response does not indicate final efficacy at that dose. Full therapeutic effect requires 4 weeks of consistent administration.

Key indicator: Increased hunger on day 7 (post-injection day) indicates the medication is effectively working during days 1-6, as retatrutide has a 6-day half-life.

Adjustment Guidelines

After 4 weeks at current dose:

  • Increase by 2mg if results are insufficient
  • Decrease by 1mg if side effects are problematic
  • Maintain dose if results are satisfactory

Maximum dose: 10mg weekly. If unsatisfied at 10mg, contact our support team.

Critical: Maintain consistent weekly dosing. Missing doses disrupts GLP-1 accumulation and reduces efficacy.

Discontinuation Protocol

Temporary Use (Goal Weight Achievement)

If discontinuing after reaching target weight:

Tapering schedule (required): Reduce dose by 1mg per week until reaching 0mg.

Do not discontinue abruptly. Abrupt cessation may cause gastrointestinal distress, rapid appetite increase, and energy fluctuations.

Long-Term Use

Retatrutide may be used indefinitely for sustained weight management, metabolic health benefits, appetite regulation, and cardiovascular protection. Continue healthy lifestyle practices and regular dosing for optimal long-term outcomes.
