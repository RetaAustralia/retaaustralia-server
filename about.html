Our Origins

AMANA Therapeutics was founded on the principle that life-changing metabolic treatments should be defined by their results, not the complexity of their administration. Having witnessed the profound metabolic and appetite-regulating benefits of Retatrutide, we also recognized a significant barrier to patient adherence: the traditional, clinical requirement of vials and manual syringes. To many, these methods felt unnecessarily complex and intimidating.

Our Commitment to Precision

We dedicated our mission to re-engineering the user experience. Inspired by the gold-standard delivery systems used by global pharmaceutical leaders, we developed a streamlined pen-injector system designed for safety, comfort, and accessibility. Every pen undergoes rigorous calibration testing and is filled by experienced professionals within a sterile, controlled environment to ensure absolute dosing accuracy. To guarantee maximum peptide potency, our products are prepared and dispatched immediately upon order, providing you with a seamless and high-purity path to your health objectives..
